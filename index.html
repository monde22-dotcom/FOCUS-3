What is Focus

Focus is a publication by The International Defence Aid Fund.The IDAF was established to support individuals and groups opposed to apartheid in South Africa and other forms of political repression in Southern Africa. It provided legal aid, published newsletters and bulletins, and supported the families of detainees and political prisoners. Focus refers to issues produced by the IDAF between 1952 and 1992. This prototype helps readers traverse the contents by names, places, and subjects. It is a simple public demo for teaching, talks, and onboarding.
