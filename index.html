What is Focus

Focus refers to the issues produced by the IDAF between the years 1952 to 1992. This website collects these issues and helps readers to access the information in a easy manner where they can traverse the contents by names, places, and subjects. This site is a simple public demo, useful for teaching, talks, and user onboarding.
